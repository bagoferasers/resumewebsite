Hello everyone, thanks for visiting my site.

My name is Colby Bailey. I am married and have two cats and dogs. I currently live in Texas. I grew up all over the place, but I consider Coos Bay, Oregon to be my hometown. After highschool, I joined the Air Force as a Radio Frequency Transmission Systems Technician. While I was in, I operated, troubleshot, maintained, and trained radio and satellite communication devices with supporting equipment in fixed and deployed locations. I was also given the opportunity to be a frontline supervisor. Now that I've honorably separated from the Military, I am pursuing a degree in Computer Science at the University of Texas in San Antonio. My expected graduation date is December 2023. I made this website to further my goal of having a job lined up by the time I graduate. I am excited to see what the future holds!
